A Team of Technical Specialists

The Sercom service team consists of highly trained engineers and senior level technicians who are capable of servicing virtually any type and make of biomedical and high-tech industrial equipment. With over 35 years of field experience, and with specialists dedicated to specific equipment categories, our staff brings unparalleled expertise to every job we're on. Today's state-of the art systems are driven by a technology that never stops evolving. Our service team keeps abreast of these technological advances and innovations by maintaining current accreditation and by attending on-going industry- and manufacturer-sponsored training seminars. In addition, our engineers and technicians are knowledgeable about current compliance, liability, and regulatory standards and requirements.
